Store and Share Your Favorite Music

Use this website to store and share your favorite music!

Be sure to share with friends and family! Click the Write Musics button to input your favorite music. You are able to put in as many songs as you would like.

Once you are done inputing music, click on the display music button to list out all the music that you, your friends, and your family have inputed.

On the display page, you are able to click on the artist and title of the song to view more details of the song.
